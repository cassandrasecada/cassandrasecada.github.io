Feature documentary about the late Raheem Reeder, a 21 year old man killed by the Tallahassee Police after a "routine traffic stop." The film follows the aftermath, his family, a group of activists, and exposes the Tallahassee Police for suspected malpractice. Release date TBA.
Directed and shot by Kiara Pomeroy. Produced by Tallahassee Community Action Committee and Kiara Pomeroy. Edited by Cassandra Secada.
