Photo by Jason LLaguno

Cassandra Secada is an editor, filmmaker, and artist from South Florida. She draws inspiration from magical realism, dreams, poetry, and eavesdropping. She feels passionately about expression as a way to connect to ourselves, others, and increase empathy in the world.

Cassandra studied at Pace University and currently lives in Brooklyn, New York. She is working as the head editor on a feature documentary, 'Raheem' (working title) directed by Kiara Pomeroy, release date TBA.
